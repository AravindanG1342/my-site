About

Aravindan Gopalakrishnan

I am a product manager based in Bangalore. Birds are how I slow down — a reason to be outdoors, patient, and paying attention.

I photograph them mostly in my backyard, neighborhood, nearby keres (lakes) and on trips where I manage to take my camera along. The joy is in simply observing birds when you can, the photos are a bonus. Hope you enjoy these photos!
